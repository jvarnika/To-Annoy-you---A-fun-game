TO ANNOY YOU is a rage-bait platformer that embraces chaos and unpredictability. The game features:


3 Difficulty Levels: Progressive challenge from learning to extreme punishment
Teleporting Doors: Watch the exit move away just as you approach it
Trolls: Boulders and asteroids attack players from top whereas Earthquakes shake your ground
Trigger Hazards: Boulders, asteroids fall, and earthquakes shake.They all work at the same time 
Audio Feedback: 6 unique sound effects that enhance every action
Mobile Ready: Full touch controls for phones and tablets

How to Play

Basic Mechanics

Get to the Door: Navigate platforms and avoid hazards to reach the glowing exit door.

Watch Out: The door might teleport away or YOU get teleported when you touch it!

Survive Hazards: Boulders will chase you, asteroids will fall on you, and earthquakes will shake your platforms. Keep moving!

Die Often: Expect lots of failures. It's part of the charm. Each death teaches you where NOT to go.

Beat 3 Levels: Progress through all 3 worlds to achieve victory.

The game tracks Deaths (not in a good way). Your goal is to complete each level with as few deaths as possible. No score system - just pure survival.
